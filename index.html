Do You Qualify?

Check Your Eligibility

The Social Care Network (SCN) is an official New York State Medicaid program. If you have Medicaid in NY State, you may qualify — anywhere in the Hudson Valley.

  • ALL WOMEN DURING PREGNANCY OR POSTPARTUM PERIODAlways Eligible
  • Food insecurity or difficulty accessing healthy meals
  • Housing instability or unsafe living conditions
  • Lack of transportation to medical or social services
  • Living with a chronic or qualifying medical condition
  • Pregnancy or postpartum (up to 12 months after birth)
  • Referred by a healthcare provider, care manager, or social worker
